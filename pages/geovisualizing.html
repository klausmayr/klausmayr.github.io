Geovisualizing Vieques

The exhibit Geovisualizing Vieques displayed maps, aerial images, and visualizations of Vieques with a focus on changes in the landscape over the last 150 years. While a lot of our work lives online, this exhibit was an opportunity to engage people in Vieques with some of our materials in-person.
